LM Installations

LM Installations Ltd, est. 1990, is an NICEIC approved Electrical Contractor in the London Area.

We have a long track record of supplying reliable high quality services to schools, academies, various council properies including important listed buildings, and a wide range of office buildings. This has provided us with a deep understanding of working in sensitive areas, and the ability to liaise with clients to minimise disruption to daily working life.

All of our on-site engineers and apprentices are DBS checked and we are happy to provide this documentation upon request.

If you would like to discuss your requirements, or request a quote, please contact us.
