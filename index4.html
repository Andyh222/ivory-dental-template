Layer 01

Electrical
Installations

The foundation of everything we do. From initial design through to certification and ongoing maintenance, we handle the full electrical lifecycle of commercial buildings — installed to BS 7671 and tested to keep you compliant.

  • Full electrical design & installation
  • Distribution board upgrades
  • Emergency & exit lighting
  • Three-phase & single-phase systems
  • EICR testing & certification
  • Consumer unit replacement
  • LED & low-voltage lighting
  • Planned & reactive maintenance
  • PAT (appliance) testing
  • Emergency lighting testing

We work around your schedule — particularly important for schools and council buildings where disruption to daily operations must be minimised.

Layer 02

Fire & Smoke
Detection

Life-safety systems designed, installed, and maintained to BS 5839. We cover the full range of premises — from schools and offices to listed buildings where surface-mounting and minimal intrusion are essential.

  • Fire alarm design to BS 5839
  • Smoke & heat detectors
  • Manual call points
  • Alarm panel installation
  • Ongoing service contracts
  • System upgrades & extensions
  • Emergency lighting integration
  • Weekly test & annual inspection

Our fire detection work has been specified for public, commercial, and residential premises — including sensitive heritage and listed building environments.

Layer 03

CCTV &
Security

Modern security systems that go beyond basic surveillance. We design and install CCTV networks, intruder detection, and monitoring systems — with the discretion and build quality that listed buildings and school campuses require.

  • IP & analogue CCTV systems
  • Internal & perimeter cameras
  • Intruder alarm systems
  • Remote monitoring setup
  • Integration with access control
  • Recorded & live footage systems
  • Maintenance & fault response
  • Site security surveys

Regularly specified for schools and academies where safeguarding requirements mean camera coverage, recording reliability, and data security are critical.

Layer 04

Door Entry &
Access Control

Control who enters your building and when. From simple audio intercom systems to fully networked access control with Urmet-certified door entry — we install systems that suit the complexity of your site.

  • Audio & video door entry
  • Urmet-certified installations
  • Proximity card & fob readers
  • Electromagnetic & electric strikes
  • Multi-zone access management
  • Visitor management integration
  • DDA-compliant door automation
  • Maintenance & emergency response

We are listed as a certified Urmet installer — providing door entry solutions for single offices through to large multi-building school campuses.

Layer 05

Data &
Communications

The infrastructure your building's digital systems depend on. Structured data cabling, telephone distribution, Wi-Fi mesh, and the low-voltage wiring that keeps modern buildings connected.

  • Structured data cabling (Cat5e/6/6a)
  • Telephone cabling & distribution
  • Wi-Fi & mesh networking
  • Server room infrastructure
  • Containment & cable management
  • Ventilation wiring
  • Audio/video & AV systems
  • Smart building systems (Hive, Nest)

From small office refreshes to full-school network infrastructure — we provide the cabling backbone that your IT systems rely on.
